Yes, we are nobili
My grand-grand-father Lamberto dropped his nobility title after WWII, as he despised the monarchy that supported the Fascists. His sons, Carlo and Fabrizio, were registered as Lambardi and so my uncles and my father Alessandro. Except for my uncle Francesco, who was registered instead as a Lambardi di San Miniato. We submitted an application to the Ministry of Interior to make our surnames all the same and set back to Lambardi di San Miniato.
We can be proud of it
Actually, we have no reasons to regret our past. Our ancestor Sebastiano Lambardi was a urban planner in Portoferraio under Napoleon and his descendants had similar jobs elsewhere. He wrote a history book, Memorie antiche e moderne dell'isola dell'Elba (IT).
Mostly harmless
Nobili are among the lowest-ranking aristocrats, right below baron (IT) and just above cavaliere ereditario (IT). Nowadays we have no power any more:

Medici blood in our veins
Cosimo de Medici had a grand-daughter Bianca, who married Guglielmo de Pazzi (IT), who had a grand-grand-grand-grand-grand-grand-grand-grand-grand-daughter Ida de Pazzi (IT). Ida had a daughter Isolina from Ubaldo Maggi (IT), a nobile of San Miniato. Isolina Maggi married Virginio Castelli and had a daughter Margherita. Lastly, Margherita Castelli married some Lamberto Lambardi, distinct from my grand-grand-father.
